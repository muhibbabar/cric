🏆 Major Cricket Tournaments

  • ICC Cricket World Cup – Held every 4 years. The biggest stage in cricket.
  • ICC T20 World Cup – The fastest and most exciting version of cricket.
  • Indian Premier League (IPL) – The richest and most glamorous T20 league.
  • The Ashes – A legendary series between England and Australia.
  • Asia Cup – Regional tournament featuring Asian teams.
